SITARAMAM

DESCRIPTION

Sita Ramam (transl.Sita and Ram) is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language period romantic drama film directed by Hanu Raghavapudi. Produced by C. Aswani Dutt, under Vyjayanthi Movies and Swapna Cinema, it starred Dulquer Salmaan and Mrunal Thakur in the lead roles roles, with Rashmika Mandanna and others in pivotal roles.
